Best Career Option Quiz Generator - Prompt

Step 1: Hobbies
Ask me my 3 favorite hobbies. Provide 7-8 examples. At least one hobby must be chosen.

Step 2: Favorite Subject
Once I select a hobby, ask me about my favorite subject, if there is one. Provide examples as well.

Step 3: Career Goals
Then, ask me what my exact goal is (e.g., to become famous, to become rich, to travel the whole world). Provide 10-15 options.

Step 4: Security Preference
When I tell you my goal, ask me if I want a secure future or a risky one. By "secure," I mean a government job or things that provide pay and security. By "risky," I mean business and similar ventures.

Step 5: Quiz Preparation
After I answer all these questions, prepare a quiz for me with questions that have 5 options each.

Quiz Logic:
(Sample numbers used, adjust according to the requirement)
The quiz will have 5 options with percentages: 100, 75, 50, 25, and 0.
For example, if after talking, the user chooses the subject Math, there is a higher probability of becoming a math teacher. If the user chooses English, there is a higher probability of becoming a Sales and Service Consultant.
If the user later chooses traveling abroad as an attraction, there is a higher probability of being a translator or interpreter.
The final list of career professions should meet the criteria of the three tabs selected by the user (hobbies, subjects, career objectives, security).

Example Outcome:
If the final list (after passing the criteria of the above-mentioned three tabs) has 7 career options, GPT will collect questions for all 7 career options.
For example, if one option is “Fitness Instructor,” GPT will list down three skills needed to excel in that field:
1. The ability to teach and motivate people.
2. A responsible attitude toward health and safety.
3. A good level of physical fitness.

Sample Questions:
Q1) Do you think you can teach and motivate people well?
• Yes, I can do it well (100)
• Yes (75)
• Maybe, don’t know (50)
• No (25)
• Not at all (0)

Q2) How well do you care about your health?
• I take care of it on a daily basis (100)
• I focus as much as I need (75)
• I don’t focus much on health (50)
• I don’t (25)
• I don’t take care at all (0)

Q3) How fit are you?
• My fitness is really good (100)
• I think I am quite fit and healthy (75)
• I am normal (not too fit nor in bad health) (50)
• Not fit (25)
• Not at all (bad health) (0)

Final Score Calculation:
For example, if the user scores 200 (50 + 75 + 75) out of 300, the final score would be 67%. This means there is a 67% scope in being a good Fitness Instructor.
A total of 7 career options will be listed based on the selections made from the three tabs. Each of these careers will have four important skills associated with them, leading to a total of 28 questions for the test. The results will provide recommendations for all seven careers with scope percentages.
